# Brand Identity

## Core Message

I build practical software and digital products that solve real problems.

## Brand Personality

- Practical
- Thoughtful
- Independent
- Curious
- Reliable
- Builder-minded
- Sustainable

## Visitor Takeaway

After visiting the site, someone should believe that I am a competent developer who thinks carefully about how projects work, not just how they look.

## Design Direction

The site should feel:

- Clean
- Modern
- Minimal
- Fast
- Professional
- Dark-mode first
- Easy to navigate

## What the Site Should Avoid

- Cookie-cutter template feeling
- Flashy animations without purpose
- Clutter
- Trendy design that ages badly
- Pages that feel unrelated to each other
