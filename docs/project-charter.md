# Personal Website Project Charter

## Vision

Build a professional website that serves as the long-term home for my work, documenting my growth as a software developer while showcasing practical software, games, projects, and future business ventures. The website should evolve throughout my career and become the central hub that connects everything I build.

---

## Mission

Help visitors quickly understand who I am, what I build, how I solve problems, and how to contact me.

---

## Primary Audience

- Recruiters
- Hiring managers
- Software engineers
- Potential employers

## Secondary Audience

- Potential clients
- Collaborators
- Friends and family

## Future Audience

- Readers
- YouTube viewers
- Customers of future businesses

---

## Core Message

I build practical software and digital products that solve real problems.

---

## Design Principles

- Simplicity over complexity.
- Fast over flashy.
- Accessibility by default.
- Mobile-first responsive design.
- Content before decoration.
- Consistency across the entire site.
- Every page should reinforce the same overall story.
- Build for longevity rather than trends.

---

## Version 1 Scope

- Home
- About
- Projects
- Resume
- Contact

---

## Not Included in Version 1

- Books
- Business pages
- YouTube content
- Advanced animations
- User accounts
- Search
- Blog comments

---

## Success Criteria

A visitor should be able to understand who I am, what I build, and how to contact me within 30 seconds of arriving on the site.

The website should be fast, accessible, responsive, professional, and easy to maintain.
