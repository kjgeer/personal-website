# Architectural Decisions

## Decision 001

### Website Purpose

The website is the professional home for Kelsey Geer.

It is a curated portfolio rather than a complete archive of every project.

**Reason**

The goal is to communicate a clear professional identity instead of overwhelming visitors.

---

## Decision 002

### Projects

Projects will be organized by purpose rather than programming language.

Games, business software, coursework, and personal projects will all appear under the Projects section.

**Reason**

Visitors care about what was built and why it matters more than what language it was written in.

---

## Decision 003

### Website First

The personal website will serve as the central hub.

GitHub, LinkedIn, and future businesses will support the website rather than replace it.

**Reason**

The website is owned and controlled by the developer and provides a stable professional identity.
